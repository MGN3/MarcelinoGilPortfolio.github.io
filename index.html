I am taking the course to obtain the official title: Superior Technician in Web Application Development. It consists of two academic years, including a professional project and a three-months internship. Why this course when you have bootcamps, online free courses and more...?

This official course is highly valued in the software development market in Spain. I have done some research and probably half the job vacants ask for this specific certificate.

In such a changing and competitive sector, having a solid background allows you to better understand the software creation process. In the bootcamps or courses lasting a few months, fewer technologies are taught and in a more superficial way. With the advent of generative AI, an in-depth knowledge of software technologies brings more value to companies
