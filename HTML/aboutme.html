Almost-lawyer?

I practically finished a Law Bachelors Degree at Universidad Europea in Madrid. I was in my last year, but, inside me, I knew since the last year that dedicating my life to that profession wouldn't be what I wanted in my life. Even though I loved most of the subjects and I had a good average grades(7-8), law was something that I wanted to understand rather than working with.

Originally, what I would have wanted to study is philosophy. But law seemed like a opportune choice as it was aa discipline that has an important philosophical side and also a profession.

In any case, I found myself really unmotivated about continuing my studies and I dropped them out. Lack of motivation wasn't the only reason to do so, but, you know, sometimes we just find ourselves envolved in personal changes that also affect our live profoundly. Drastically, I started designing and working on my own in a 300 square meter piece of land, following the principles of permaculture, with the goal of making a living out of it.

Reflections

And technology is also about that.
